Simple Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix dry ingredients.

2. In another bowl, whisk wet ingredients.

3. Combine both mixtures until smooth.

4. Heat a pan over medium heat.

5. Pour 1/4 cup batter onto pan.

6. Flip when bubbles form, cook until golden.
